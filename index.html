What AppFreeze Pro does

Everything the Lite monitor shows you — plus the power to act on it.

Screenshot: img/switch-apps.png
(click a row to bring an app forward / hide it)

Switch & hide apps in a click

Click any app in the list to bring it forward, or click it again to hide it — a quick way to jump between what’s running, right from the menu bar.

Screenshot: img/freeze-toggles.png
(Freeze All / Freeze Selected switches)

One-click freezing

Freeze every background app at once, or auto-freeze a remembered set. Suspended apps stop using the CPU instantly — no quitting, no losing your work.

Screenshot: img/smart-mode.png
(app thaws on focus, refreezes on leave)

Smart auto-freeze

Apps thaw the moment you switch to them and refreeze when you leave. You get the battery savings without ever thinking about it.

Screenshot: img/energy-monitor.png
(per-app %CPU with Low/Med/High dot)

Live energy monitor

See real per-app CPU usage with a colour-coded Low/Medium/High indicator, so you know exactly which apps are draining your battery.

Screenshot: img/shortcut-login.png
(global shortcut & Launch at Login)

Shortcut & Launch at Login

Trigger freezing from anywhere with a global shortcut, and have AppFreeze ready in your menu bar the moment you log in.

Screenshot: img/safety.png
(active app / Finder / system protected)

Safe by design

AppFreeze never suspends the app you’re using, Finder, or critical system processes — your Mac stays responsive at all times.
